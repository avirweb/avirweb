A Tribute to Iconic Design: The Beosystem 9000c

Bang & Olufsen’s Beosystem 9000c is a stunning reimagination of the classic Beosound 9000 CD player from the 1990s, complemented by the cutting-edge Beolab 28 loudspeakers. Limited to just 200 units, this system marks the second installment in Bang & Olufsen’s Recreated Classics series.

‍

‍

The Essence of Timeless Craftsmanship

At the heart of the Beosystem 9000c lies a meticulously restored Beosound 9000 CD player, originally designed by David Lewis in 1996. Each unit undergoes a thorough inspection and restoration process by skilled technicians in Struer, Denmark. The modern refurb features a striking Cosmic Black finish on the aluminum back plate, juxtaposed with a Natural Aluminum control panel, enhancing the graphic edge of the original design.

‍

‍

Unmatched Sound Quality and Modern Features

The Beosystem 9000c, priced at £45,000 (€50,000 / $55,000), promises a powerful listening experience with Beolab 28 speakers. Equipped with Dolby Atmos support and various wireless connectivity options such as Airplay 2, Chromecast, and Bluetooth 5.0, this blend of classic design and modern technology ensures that your existing Bang & Olufsen system is enhanced with further longevity beyond just the physical life of your components. Your system is ready for 2024 and beyond.

‍

‍

Innovating Connectivity: The Beoconnect Core

Transforming Legacy Systems

The new Bang & Olufsen Beoconnect Core is designed to make streaming accessible with almost any Bang & Olufsen audio system, converting older speakers into a wireless system. This streaming box is equipped with various connections, including USB, Powerlink, and HDMI with ARC or eARC, ensuring compatibility with a wide range of systems, even those used for decades.

‍

‍

Seamless Integration and Modern Design

Based on the Bang & Olufsen Mozart Platform, the Beoconnect Core offers a compact and elegant design with a bead-blasted aluminum housing. It integrates seamlessly into any modern living environment while supporting advanced streaming technologies. With features like Line In for connecting record players and third-party screens, the Beoconnect Core embodies Bang & Olufsen’s goal to build products that last a lifetime.

‍

‍

A Future-Proof Solution

As Michael Henriksson, Vice President of Product Marketing at Bang & Olufsen, states, "Beoconnect Core connects our past with our future and comes with our interchangeable Mozart streaming module that can be easily upgraded to the latest technology." This ensures that beloved Bang & Olufsen products continue to deliver memorable moments for years to come.
