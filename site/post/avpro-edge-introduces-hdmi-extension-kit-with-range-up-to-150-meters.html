AVPro Edge Introduces HDMI Extension Kit With Range Up to 150 Meters

Integrators now have a clear solution for passing HDMI signals through punch-down blocks and cable splices.

Original post by Residential Systems.

AVPro Edge has introduced the AC-EX150-BKT, an HDMI extension kit developed to provide integrators with 150 meters of range when using HDMI connectivity. The new kit offers 50 meters of additional reach when compared to conventional solutions, and effectively overcomes key challenges encountered by AV integrators seeking to affordably leverage legacy network infrastructure to transmit HDMI signals over long runs. Integrators now have a clear solution for passing HDMI signals through punch-down blocks and cable splices.

“The AC-EX150-BKT is the ultimate solution for AV integrators seeking to overcome infrastructure and distance obstacles that hamper conventional HDMI extension technologies,” explains Sam Metivier, product manager for AVPro Edge. “This kit delivers a stable HDMI signal over long runs and is easy to use — creating opportunities for integrators to satisfy their clients without the significant cost of replacing legacy category wiring.”

The AC-EX150-BKT kit features a compact transmitter and receiver plus mounting and wiring accessories. The kit retrofits almost any Category cable-based infrastructure and meets 4K, HDMI 2.0 specifications using Cat 5e or better cabling, making this a versatile tool for AV integrators:

  • Overcome infrastructure obstacles such as dated Category cabling or splices, couplers, and punch-down blocks
  • Can be used with most legacy 8-conductor Category cabling
  • Long-range transmission capability; up to 150 meters
  • EDID management
  • 4K to 1080p Downscaling
  • CEC friendly
  • RS-232 and IR extension

AVPro Edge products are backed by a 10-year advanced-replacement warranty.
