Wisdom Audio S57i: A Subwoofer That Fits Anywhere and Sounds Amazing

If you’ve been searching for a subwoofer that balances stellar performance with a low-profile design, the Wisdom Audio S57i might be just what you need.

Introduced at the CEDIA Expo 2024, this in-wall (or in-ceiling) subwoofer delivers deep, punchy bass without needing to sacrifice space or aesthetics. It’s designed to blend seamlessly into your room while still packing a serious audio punch.

‍

‍

What Makes It Special?

The standout feature of the S57i is its Transmission Line (RTL) design. Without getting too technical, this design helps deliver really clean, powerful bass that doesn’t feel boomy or out of place. It also ensures smooth sound across a wide frequency range, from the deep thumps at 25Hz to the higher bass notes at 130Hz. Essentially, it’s great for both movie nights and music playlists.

It’s built to fit neatly into standard 2x4 wall spaces (or ceilings) with a depth of just 4 inches. So, whether you're working on a custom home theater or trying to keep things minimalist in a living room, it won’t stick out—literally or figuratively.

Key Features You’ll Actually Care About

Here are the basics that matter:

  • Flat Frequency Response: Deep bass down to 25Hz for a full sound experience.
  • Compact Build: Fits in-wall or in-ceiling with only 4 inches of depth.
  • High Performance: Uses a 10-inch woofer for precise, impactful sound.
  • DSP Ready: Requires some digital sound processing for setup, meaning you can fine-tune it to your space.
  • Power Handling: Handles up to 500 watts, so it won’t struggle with demanding audio.

It’s designed for people who want high-quality sound but don’t want big, bulky speakers taking up floor space. Plus, it blends in with its grille, so it stays subtle in any room.

Specs at a Glance

  • Frequency Response: 25Hz – 130Hz (±3dB)
  • Peak Power: 500W
  • Sensitivity: 84dB
  • Max Output: 108dB
  • Dimensions: 53.27” x 16” x 4.13” (HxWxD)

It’s also lightweight (for a subwoofer) at 40 lbs, making it easier to install than many alternatives.

‍

‍

Why It Stands Out

The S57i isn’t just about looking good in a wall—it’s about sounding great too. According to Ron Rouse, Wisdom Audio’s VP of Sales and Marketing, this product addresses a big gap in the market for slim-profile subwoofers that still deliver top-tier audio. If you’ve ever felt like most in-wall options compromise sound quality for design, this one aims to fix that.

When and Where Can You Get It?

The S57i will start shipping in December 2024. It’s worth considering if you’re planning a home theater or multi-room audio system where design and sound matter equally. Whether you're a casual movie buff or an audiophile, this subwoofer offers a solid balance of performance and practicality.

It’s definitely worth keeping on your radar if you’re updating or planning a high-end sound setup.
