Grimani Systems MP-84: Seamless Design

Built on decades of acoustic innovation, this speaker provides detailed, room-filling sound without visible hardware disrupting your space.

It is a favorite among homeowners looking for luxury audio that blends quietly into their environment.

‍

Minimal Visual Impact, Maximum Audio Presence

At a glance, the MP-84 almost disappears. Its slim 4 inch grille, offered in either round or square, sits flush against the ceiling. The finish is paintable, allowing it to match any room style. Behind that small footprint lies a powerful speaker system including an eight inch woofer and a two inch tweeter housed in a concealed aluminum enclosure.

This compact design delivers clarity, range, and deep bass that reaches well below what most in-ceiling speakers can manage. The MP-84 can easily support demanding playback for home theaters, media rooms, and full-home music systems.

‍

‍

Uniform Sound Across the Room

One of the most refined features of the MP-84 is its wide dispersion capability. Instead of focusing sound into a narrow listening area, the speaker projects evenly in all directions. This means your music or film soundtrack sounds consistent whether you are at the center of the room or walking along its edges.

This makes the MP-84 particularly effective in larger or more open spaces where other speakers might struggle to maintain balance.

‍

Intelligent Audio Optimization

Unlike passive speakers, the MP-84 includes an integrated digital signal processor that works with a rack-mounted amplifier delivering 320 watts. This system allows an expert to calibrate the sound based on the specific acoustics of your room.

Its monitoring and diagnostic tools operate over your home network, enabling remote fine-tuning and system checks without the need for in-person service calls. The result is long-term performance that adapts to your environment over time.

‍

‍

Built for Real Homes, Inside and Out

The MP-84’s enclosure is made of precision-engineered aluminum and is rated for outdoor use. It is well suited for installations in kitchens, bathrooms, patios, or even high-humidity environments. Wherever you place it, the system maintains consistent performance and long service life.

‍

An Elevated Listening Experience for the Design-Conscious Homeowner

With an MSRP of five thousand dollars per speaker, the MP-84 belongs in homes where quality and discretion matter equally. It suits those who value technical sophistication but do not want to see it. Whether you are creating a private cinema, expanding whole-home audio, or simply upgrading a favorite room, the MP-84 offers unmatched balance between performance and subtlety.
