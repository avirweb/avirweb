Sony VPL-XW7000ES: Elevating Home Cinema

At the pinnacle of home cinema technology stands the Sony VPL-XW7000ES projector, powered by the advanced X1 Ultimate for projector processor. This marvel ensures each scene is depicted with pinpoint accuracy, transforming every piece of entertainment into an immersive, vibrant, and luminous spectacle. With Sony's leading-edge processing, each movie and show morphs into an experience you won't forget.

Unmatched Brightness and Precision

With a sticker price of $27,999.99, the VPL-XW7000ES dazzles with its 3,200 lumens brightness, thanks to a durable laser light source. This brilliance guarantees vivid images across expansive screens, making the cinematic experience at home feel as real as possible, with natural colors shining through even in the brightest rooms.

At its heart is the renowned X1™ Ultimate processor, delivering unparalleled image processing. Paired with a brand-new Native 4K SXRD panel, the projector offers complete 4K resolution at 3840 x 2160 pixels, capturing every detail with crystal clear sharpness.

Revolutionary Color and Contrast

With its Wide Dynamic Range Optics, Sony sets new standards for color and contrast. This technology not only allows for a sleek design but also ensures meticulous light control, hitting a 95% DCI-P3 wide color gamut. Additional features like dual database processing, object-based super resolution, and HDR remastering meticulously analyze and refine the picture, producing images with life-like depth and realism.

Enhanced Dynamic HDR and Sharp Focus

In HDR enhancement, the VPL-XW7000ES stands out, enhancing contrast in sync with laser output for radiant 4K HDR images. Its Digital Focus Optimizer guarantees unparalleled clarity, even at the edges, while the Picture Position Memory remembers your preferences for a personalized viewing journey.

Crafted for the Ultimate Viewing Experience

Sony's dedication to pushing the envelope is reflected in the VPL-XW7000ES's sleek and modern design. Approximately 20% smaller and 30% lighter than previous models, it integrates effortlessly into any decor. Supporting IMAX Enhanced content, it delivers enthralling visuals that elevate your projector screen experience, immersing you deeper into the cinematic world.

The VPL-XW7000ES is more than a technological masterpiece; it symbolizes Sony's commitment to the future of entertainment. Embracing a mercury-free design and minimizing the use of virgin plastic, Sony demonstrates its pledge to sustainable innovation. This projector is not just an addition to your home entertainment; it's a gateway to unparalleled 4K HDR adventures, designed to dazzle and amaze.
