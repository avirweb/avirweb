McIntosh and Sonus Faber Partner with Jeep and Lamborghini

Posted on 
June 12, 2024

Top-Tier Audio at Home and On the Road: McIntosh and Sonus Faber, Partnerships with Jeep and Lamborghini

When it comes to luxury audio, brands like McIntosh and Sonus faber are renowned for delivering exceptional sound quality. Their recent collaborations with world-class vehicles exemplify how one can maintain the high-fidelity audio experience enjoyed at home, even while on the road.

‍

‍

Detailed Breakdown of McIntosh’s MX1200 Entertainment System

24-Channel Premium Amplifier: The MX1200 Entertainment System in the 2024 Jeep Wagoneer S Launch Edition features a 24-channel amplifier, distributing up to 1200 watts of power. This ensures each of the 19 speakers gets the power it needs, resulting in superior sound quality and power distribution. Such a robust setup is essential for maintaining clarity and richness in the audio experience.

Speakers and Placement: The system includes a variety of speakers, such as four 6- x 9-inch door speakers, coaxial speakers, and a 12-inch dual voice coil subwoofer. These speakers are strategically placed to create an immersive sound experience. For example, rear speakers mounted in the liftgate play low enough to blend perfectly with the subwoofer, enhancing the overall sound stage.

McIntosh Technologies: McIntosh’s patented technologies, including Power Guard and LD/HP (Low Distortion/High Performance), play a crucial role in eliminating distortion and maintaining audio clarity even at high volumes. Power Guard prevents clipping, which can damage speakers and degrade sound quality, while LD/HP ensures pristine sound reproduction across all frequencies.

‍

‍

Sonus faber’s Integration in the Lamborghini Revuelto

Collaboration with Lamborghini: Sonus faber and Lamborghini have joined forces to create an unparalleled audio experience in the new Revuelto. This collaboration involved close cooperation between Sonus faber’s R&D team and Lamborghini’s designers, overcoming numerous challenges to integrate high-quality audio seamlessly into the vehicle’s design.

Material and Design Choices: The choice of materials, such as natural fiber composites and phase plugs, significantly impacts the sound quality and aesthetics of the audio system. These materials were specifically chosen to enhance the acoustic performance and provide a luxurious look that complements Lamborghini’s design ethos.

Speaker Configuration: The Revuelto features a seven-speaker setup, including tweeters, mid-woofers, and full-range speakers. Each speaker is meticulously placed to deliver an exceptional sound experience, with two full-range speakers positioned behind the pilot and co-pilot for an immersive surround effect.

Amplifier and DSP: The system includes a Full Class D amplifier delivering up to 750 watts of power, paired with a powerful DSP. This combination ensures smooth transitions between electrical signals and acoustical tones, providing high-fidelity sound and a rich audio experience.

‍

Market Position and Future Trends

The partnerships between McIntosh, Sonus faber, and leading automotive brands like Jeep and Lamborghini are shaping expectations in the luxury audio market. These collaborations highlight the demand for high-quality audio experiences both at home and on the road, setting new standards for sound performance in luxury vehicles and residential settings.
