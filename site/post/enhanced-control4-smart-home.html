Enhanced Control4 Smart Home OS 3.3.0

‍Color Outside the Lines
Control4 has introduced exciting new features that enhance your smart home experience.

Set the Mood, Control the Ambiance
With the new built-in color control for LED lighting in OS 3.3.0, you can now illuminate your home with a full spectrum of colors. The innovative color wheel feature lets you create and save favorite color presets for instant enjoyment, providing whole-home ambiance control.

‍

‍

Better, Faster, and Sooner

We understand that time is precious, so we’ve improved the speed of remote connections. With the latest update, the Control4 App for iOS and Android now offers faster remote access, making it as quick as if you were right there at home.

Favorites at Your Fingertips

Your home at your fingertips — literally. The Control4 App now supports the Apple Watch, providing easy access to your selected Favorites. Control your home conveniently from your wrist and enjoy the seamless integration of technology into your daily routine.

How Do I?

Navigating new features can be daunting, but Control4 has you covered. We’ve released a series of quick, informative videos showing you how to interact with and customize your smart home. From uploading custom backgrounds to favoriting devices and sharing media, these videos will help you use your system like a pro. Watch and learn from the multi-part series here.

‍

EVERY INTERACTION IS BETTER

Experience Unparalleled Control
Control4 OS 3 offers over a thousand new features and interaction enhancements, thoughtfully redesigned to simplify your smart home experience. Whether you’re using the Halo Touch, Halo Remote, in-wall or tabletop touchscreens, or the Control4 OS app on your phone, you’ll enjoy unparalleled control.

‍

‍

More Personalized Than Ever Before

Make your smart home uniquely yours with OS 3’s unmatched personalization capabilities. Mark frequently used rooms as favorites, customize wallpapers, and add your most-used devices and media to the main “room view” for quick access.

‍

Simplicity at Its Finest

The intuitive OS 3 interface provides easy access and control for all devices across your home. Enjoy at-a-glance views of your entire home, allowing you to see the status of your security system, smart door locks, shades, and lights all on a single screen.

‍

‍

Add Favorites for Your Convenience

Favorites let you personalize your smart home experience. Easily arrange, remove, and favorite your most-used devices, media, and scenes for quick access on your phone, touchscreen, or Neeo remote.

‍

Wallpapers to Complement

Choose from curated wallpapers designed by top interior designers or upload your own photos to personalize the look of your control interfaces across phones, tablets, touch screens, and TV screens.

‍

‍

Reimagined Entertainment Experience

OS 3 icons visually represent the status of connected devices, allowing you to instantly see whether a door is locked, a fan is on, or the garage is open. The Comfort screen provides weather details and lets you adjust the temperature with a single tap. The Active Media Bar shows what’s playing around the house, giving you clear and quick control of your home entertainment.

‍

Providing You the Best Experience from Any Interface You Use

Whether you're using a mobile device, a Control4 touchscreen, or a handheld remote, the OS 3 interface is designed to be fast, intuitive, and user-friendly.

‍

Benefits

Unmatched Personalization and Control

The updates in Control4 OS 3.3.0 bring a new level of personalization to your smart home. The built-in color control for LED lighting allows you to set the perfect ambiance with ease. The faster remote connections ensure that you can manage your home efficiently, no matter where you are. The support for Apple Watch provides unparalleled convenience, putting control of your home right on your wrist.

‍

Enhanced User Experience

Control4 OS 3 offers an intuitive interface with over a thousand new features designed to simplify your smart home experience. The ability to favorite rooms and devices, customize wallpapers, and manage your home with a single touch makes everyday interactions seamless and enjoyable.

‍

Comprehensive Control at Your Fingertips

Whether you’re at home or on the go, the Control4 system ensures that you have comprehensive control over all your smart devices. The updated app and interfaces provide clear, quick access to your home’s systems, enhancing security, comfort, and entertainment.

By implementing these updates, Control4 continues to offer a robust, user-friendly smart home experience, keeping you connected and in control like never before.
